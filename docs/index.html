Spoke uses your Mac's built-in voices. Quality varies dramatically — here's what to expect.

Recommended

Siri 2 (US)

Smooth, natural reading with great pacing and clean handling of acronyms.

Siri 1 (UK)

Good quality, but noticeably less smooth — occasional awkward pauses can break the flow.

Samantha Enhanced

A smaller, older voice — shows what you're stuck with without Siri voices installed.
